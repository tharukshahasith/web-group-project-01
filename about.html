About DriveEase

Serving Sri Lanka

Since 2012

DriveEase started in 2012 with a small fleet of 12 cars and a single office in Colombo. Our founders wanted to make vehicle rental simple and affordable for everyone — whether you're a tourist exploring the island or a local who needs a vehicle for a few days.

Over the years we have grown our fleet to over 150 vehicles and opened pickup points in Kandy, Galle, Negombo, Trincomalee and several other cities. We now serve thousands of customers every year.

  • Over 12 years of experience in vehicle rental
  • 150+ well maintained vehicles
  • 25+ pickup locations across Sri Lanka
  • 12,000+ satisfied customers
  • Rated 4.8/5 on Google Reviews
