Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle? ▼
You need a valid driving licence (Sri Lankan or International), a National Identity Card or Passport. Foreign visitors must hold an International Driving Permit. You will also need a debit or credit card for the security deposit.
Is insurance included in the price? ▼
Yes. All our rentals include comprehensive third-party insurance and 24/7 roadside assistance at no extra cost. There is no additional insurance charge.
Can I cancel or change my booking? ▼
Yes. You can cancel or modify your booking for free up to 48 hours before the pickup time. Cancellations within 48 hours may be charged a small fee. Contact us to make changes.
What is the minimum age to rent? ▼
The minimum age is 21 years for standard cars and SUVs, with at least one year of driving experience. For luxury vehicles the minimum age is 25 years.
When do I pay for the rental? ▼
Payment is made at the pickup location. We accept cash (LKR), Visa and Mastercard. No online payment is required when you book — the booking just reserves the vehicle for you.
Can I pick up and drop off at different locations? ▼
Yes, one-way rentals are available between our main locations (Colombo, Kandy, Galle). Please mention this in the special requests section when booking, or call us to confirm availability. A small one-way surcharge may apply.
